Community Rules

Follow these rules to keep the NYCRP VIP community safe, fun, and inclusive.

Respect Everyone

No harassment, hate speech, or discrimination is allowed.

Stay In-Character

Keep roleplay in the right channels and avoid out-of-character disruption.

Use Channels Correctly

Post content in the appropriate channels and follow pinned instructions.

No Exploits

Cheating, exploiting, or abusing server systems is prohibited.

Honor Staff Decisions

Staff decisions are final. Appeal respectfully only through official channels.
